# Every

An AI-native media + software company founded by Dan Shipper. Publishes the *Source Code* column (mostly written by Katie Parrott) and ships a portfolio of [[concepts/agent-native]] consumer apps: [[projects/cora]], [[projects/monologue]], Sparkle, and Spiral.

## Eating their own cooking

Every famously runs internal ops on four custom agents:

- **Anton** — daily prioritization (built by COO Brandon Gell)
- **Max** — meeting transcripts → tasks
- **Strategy Interviewer** — compressed multi-week OKR planning into {{source:parrott-25-person-four-agents}}two days{{/source}}
- **Campaign Reporter** — daily growth scorecard built by Austin Tedesco {{source:parrott-25-person-four-agents}}end-to-end from terminal in Claude Code{{/source}}

All four sit on top of three interconnected Notion DBs: calendar, tasks, strategy. {{source:parrott-25-person-four-agents}}*"Cross-references between these are the entire substrate."*{{/source}}

## Programs

- **Agent Native Camp** — first event where product leaders shared implementations.
- {{source:notion-custom-agents-camp}}**Custom Agents Camp** (with Notion, April 3 2026, 1-hour virtual){{/source}} — 500+ subscribers learning to build their own agents; Brian Lovin (Notion) co-hosted.
- **Compound Engineering Camp** — Klaassen's methodology extended beyond engineering.

## Stance

> {{source:parrott-25-person-four-agents}}*"Describe the outcome, not the steps. Tell the AI what you want to accomplish and let it figure out the implementation. Over-prescribing tends to confuse the model."*{{/source}}

## Building a serious editorial system on top of Claude

Most of Parrott's recent column — and a lot of Every's published methodology — is about treating AI-assisted writing as a discipline, not a shortcut:

- {{source:parrott-claude-every-standards}}*"When you can teach taste to a machine, you're forced to make it legible for yourself."*{{/source}} The Every standards system distinguishes **structural patterns** from **core principles** ({{source:parrott-claude-every-standards}}optimistic realism, intellectual generosity, conversational authority{{/source}}). See [[concepts/taste-as-skill]].
- {{source:parrott-board-games-and-ai}}Board games as a teach{{/source}}: components → moves → sequencing → victory conditions. See [[concepts/compound-engineering]].
- {{source:parrott-writing-with-ai-harder}}Multi-stage writing pipeline (brainstorm → interview → outline → draft → review){{/source}} with specialized critic personas. The rebuttal to *"any portion done by AI is less thinking done by you."*

## Eats its own dog food on tooling too

Every ships **Proof**, an editor that {{source:parrott-ai-autopilot}}tracks which words came from humans vs. machines{{/source}} — surfacing provenance is the design-level fix Parrott proposes for [[concepts/ai-autopilot]]. And Every staff are the most-documented Claw users in the wild — see [[projects/openclaw]] for Margot, Klont, Zosia, Pip.
