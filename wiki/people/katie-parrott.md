# Katie Parrott

Staff writer and AI editorial lead at [[projects/every]]. Author of the Source Code column's most-cited pieces on [[concepts/agent-native]] software, including the {{source:parrott-four-apps}}lessons-from-four-apps writeup of Every's first Agent Native Camp{{/source}} and the {{source:parrott-25-person-four-agents}}breakdown of how Every runs internal operations on four custom Notion agents{{/source}}.

## Recurring lens

Parrott consistently frames agent-native work in operational terms: not *can the agent do it?* but *what does running a company on agents actually look like?* — meeting transcripts becoming tasks, OKR planning compressed from weeks to two days, COO traffic-control offloaded to an agent named Anton.

Her shorthand for an agent-native app: {{source:parrott-four-apps}}"Claude Code in a trench coat."{{/source}}
