# Every × Notion: Custom Agents Camp

**Aired:** April 3, 2026. **Format:** 1-hour virtual camp, recording available. **Hosts:** [[projects/every]] + Notion. **Notion rep:** Brian Lovin.

## Promise

> *"Learn how to put custom agents to work inside your business."*

Attendees receive templates for immediate implementation.

## What's covered

- Walk-through of {{source:parrott-25-person-four-agents}}the four agents powering daily operations at Every (Anton, Max, the Strategy Interviewer, the Campaign Reporter){{/source}}.
- Brian Lovin demonstrates how the Notion team itself uses custom agents.
- Notion's roadmap for the custom-agent product surface.
- Practical templates the audience can adapt.

## Audience

Business leaders and teams looking to offload operational logistics onto AI agents. No prerequisites stated.
