Personal field notes from AI Dev Con 2026.

**The Second Wave.** 2025 was about what is possible. 2026 is about what is reliable and secure at scale — regulatory pressure (data residency), latency reality (fraud detection etc.), and data gravity (moving siloed data to one place).

**Human characteristics that matter.**
- Tinkering — jump in, build, figure it out
- Critical thinking — what to debug, where, when, how
- Taste — is something good or not; requires looking at many patterns and developing judgment
- Prioritization — token-maxing discourse misses the point; time management is what matters
- Perspectives — models are not the problem; knowing what to do with them is

**Building blocks of agentic dev.**
- Agentic blocks: LLM, RAG, agentic workflows, evals, error analysis, memory, context
- Non-agentic blocks: UI, auth, DB
- Prod blocks: user empathy, rapid prototyping
